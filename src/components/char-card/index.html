Biography

Thalion grew up in the misty highlands, training under the watchful eye of the order of steel. Known for his unwavering courage and steadfast loyalty, he protects the weak and seeks glory in battle.

Favored weapon: Greatsword. Known companion: Raven named Korr. Hobbies: Storytelling and forging small trinkets.
